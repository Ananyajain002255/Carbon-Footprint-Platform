🌿 Why Carbon Footprint Matters?

🌡 Climate Change

High carbon emissions contribute to global warming and climate change.

🌊 Rising Sea Levels

Increased temperatures cause glaciers to melt and sea levels to rise.

🌳 Deforestation

Forest destruction reduces nature's ability to absorb carbon dioxide.

♻ Tips To Reduce Carbon Footprint

  • 🚲 Use bicycles for short distances.
  • 🚌 Prefer public transportation.
  • 💡 Save electricity and water.
  • 🌳 Plant more trees.
  • 🔋 Use renewable energy sources.
  • 🛍 Reduce plastic consumption.
